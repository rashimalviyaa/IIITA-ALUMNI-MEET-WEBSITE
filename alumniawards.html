Alumni Awards - IIIT Allahabad

The Alumni Association proudly recognizes the achievements of distinguished alumni, friends, and students of IIIT Allahabad by presenting awards at its annual Alumni Awards Banquet. Past honorees have included outstanding educators, successful business people, prominent scientists, sports and entertainment personalities, and ordinary people with extraordinary devotion to IIITA.

YOU can be a part of IIITA’s biggest night of the year! Submit an online nomination today to recommend an outstanding member of the IIITA University family for an alumni award.

National Awards

  • Distinguished Alumnus/Alumna

    The Distinguished Alumnus/Alumna Award recognizes IIITA alumni for extraordinary distinction and success in a career field and who have demonstrated exceptional and sustained community leadership. The award recipient should have achieved state, regional, or national recognition. Preference will be given to those who, while enrolled at IIITA, were active participants in the life of the university.

    Nominate

  • Distinguished Young Alumnus/Alumna

    The Young Alumnus/Alumna Award recognizes established and future leaders among IIITA’s young alumni. The nominee must have graduated within the past ten years, be less than 40 years of age, attained a level of distinction in their chosen field of work, demonstrated a commitment to the service of others and to the university, and shown the potential for future leadership/distinction. Preference will be given to those who, while enrolled at IIITA, were active participants in the life of the university.
